RETURN POLICY
Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned. Shipping costs for returns are the customer's responsibility unless the item arrived damaged or defective.

REFUND POLICY
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Orders that have not yet been delivered cannot be refunded — they must be cancelled instead if the request is made before shipping.

SHIPPING POLICY
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days and costs an additional 200 rupees. We currently ship only within India. Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day.

CANCELLATION POLICY
Orders can be cancelled free of charge within 1 hour of placing the order. After 1 hour, if the order has not yet shipped, a cancellation fee of 50 rupees applies. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled — customers must wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

WARRANTY POLICY
Electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects. Warranty does not cover physical damage, water damage, or normal wear and tear. To claim warranty, customers need their original order number and proof of purchase.

DAMAGED ITEM POLICY
If an item arrives damaged, customers must report it within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. Damaged items qualify for a full refund or free replacement, customer's choice. No return shipping cost applies for damaged items.
